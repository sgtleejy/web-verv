FAQ

Everything you need to know about the product and how it works. Can't find an answer? Chat to our team.

What is included in the free trial?

You'll get 250 credits during the free trial, which covers roughly 100 images or 25 videos. The trial is active for 3 days from the moment you begin.

How do I start creating videos?

Start by uploading your product photo and choosing an art direction to set the visual tone. Add any prompt details you want, then generate your images. Each run gives you 4 variations, and you can turn any of them into videos or refine them further with additional prompts.

What are "Art Directions"?

Art Directions are VERV's handpicked templates curated by creative directors and real humans, that apply trending, culture-driven visual styles to your product photos and videos. This is what sets VERV apart from traditional AI creation tools. With 50+ options, you can achieve editorial-grade, on-trend results in one click without the "generic AI look" or aesthetic guesswork.

How's VERV better than other traditional AI image and video creation tools?

VERV gives Ecommerce brands the tools to create studio-quality ads and product photography with true art direction. Where other AI tools produce generic, "AI-looking" results, VERV delivers brand-consistent, art-directed visuals that look and feel real.

How are credits spent?

Generations and edits both cost 10 credits per set of 4 images. Converting any image into a video format also requires 10 credits.

Is it safe to use VERV-generated creatives for commercial purposes?

Absolutely. Every creative you generate with VERV is commercially safe and ready for use immediately.

Will unused credits roll over to the next month?

No, unused credits will not roll over.

Do you have an unlimited or annual plan available?

We don't offer an unlimited or annual plan at the moment.

When can I cancel my plan?

You can cancel your plan at any time. Your subscription will be active until the end of the billing cycle.
